What's inside the hub?

📚 The Library

Worksheets and activities across ten themes — from anxiety and anger to friendships and resilience. Preview them online or download to edit.

Quick, calming, in-the-moment activities: breathing bubbles, mood check-ins, kind-word scrambles and journal prompts. Nothing is saved or tracked — just play.
