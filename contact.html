If you need help right now Please don't use this form in an emergency. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 999. You can also call Childline free on 0800 1111, Samaritans on 116 123, or text SHOUT to 85258 — all free, day and night.

Possible questions

Are the resources really free?

Yes — everything in the library is completely free to preview, download, edit and use with the young people you support.

Can I edit the resources for a specific young person?

Please do! That's exactly what they're for. Resources are provided in editable formats wherever possible so you can adapt them to each young person's needs.

Who makes these resources?

I do — a youth advocate, drawing on personal experience of real one-to-one wellbeing work with young people. They are supportive tools, not medical or therapeutic advice.

Can I request a resource on a specific topic?

Absolutely — that's one of the main reasons this form exists. Send your idea over and it may appear in the library's "recently added" section.

Do the games save or track anything?

No. The games are just for fun in the moment — nothing you tap, type or choose is saved, stored or sent anywhere.

Can I make a referral through this website?

No — this website can't accept referrals or provide direct support. If a young person needs help, please contact their school, GP, or the support services listed on the About page.
